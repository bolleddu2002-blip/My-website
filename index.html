Hey there! I’m Pradeep Bolleddu. I’m an Applied Scientist at InstaWorks Inc and previously worked with Oliv AI as an AI Data Science Intern. I build products with AI & modern technology. My journey includes 3D Computer Vision work at UMASS Chan Medical School, LLM and AI solutions at Third AI Automation, and research during my degrees at the University of Massachusetts (Master's) and IIT Ropar (B.Tech). During my bachelor’s at IIT Ropar, I worked with Dr. Ekta Singla on an Autonomous WardBot. For my master’s at UMass I worked under Prof. Julia Fang, and I collaborated closely with Prof. Scott Field and Prof. Vijaya Chalivendra on campus projects.

I’m passionate about blending research-grade models with production systems — from medical imaging and prognosis models to LLM-powered retrieval systems. I enjoy open-source work, designing experiments, and shipping solutions that make measurable impact.
